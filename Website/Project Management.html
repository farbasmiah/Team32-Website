Record of Progress

COMP204P: OCT-DEC

A record of the progress of our project shall be outlined in a table below, separated into the two halves of the project.

The first half of the project has been primarily aimed at research and prototyping. We have carried our sufficient research into what our clients want and presented them in the form of MoSCoW style requirements. Some prototypes have been created on the user interface with Use Cases and HCI analysis carried out on them to ensure the requirements have been fulfilled. As well as this, we have produced some scripts to be run on the server side of the web application to be produced.
